Privacy Policy

At Sky Mountain, we value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you access or use our property management platform.

1. Information We Collect
We collect the types of information as below in order to provide and improve our services. Personal Data: Our collection of personal data will include name, e-mail address, telephone number, and all other details, including other forms of contact, with which you register an account or place a property listing or request for real estate services. Payment Data: Billing information and payment options when you transact on our site. Property Data: Information concerning properties that have been posted, for instance images, descriptions, and price information. Usage Information: Information about how you are using our site includes pages viewed, properties viewed or listed, and length of duration on the site. Device Information: Information about the device you are using to access our site, such as IP address, browser, and operating system.

2. How We Use Your Information
The collected information will be put to the following uses: Creation and administration of your account. Facilitating listings, inquiries and sales pertaining to property. Payment for real estate transactions or services. Sending important updates, alerts and marketing materials. Optimization and studies regarding the experience of use via monitoring behaviour. Legal compliance and safeguarding our platform.

3. Sharing and Disclosure of Information
We are not in the habit of giving out your personal data to a third party, except in the following cases: Service Providers: We will share with our authorized third-party service providers which help us in the process of the payment processing, hosting, and data analysis of your information. Legal Compliance: We can divulge your information except as provided in law and proper request from government or other competent authority. Business Transfers: Your information will be transferred to others under business transfer where bank is involved in merging or acquiring business or selling business.

4. Data Protection
We use industry-standard security for protection of your personal information from unauthorized access, loss or misuse. But, no one can be 100 percent sure of the information transmitted over the internet, so we cannot ensure complete security.

5. Your Rights
You have the right to: Access and review the personal information we hold about you. Request corrections or updates to your personal information. Request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal obligations. Opt out of marketing emails by following the instructions in the marketing emails to unsubscribe.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
We use cookies and related technologies to learn about you and what you do while browsing our website. Browser settings will allow you to turn off the use of cookies. If this is done, it will affect your use of certain features of the platform.
