About this Property

This stylish 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom apartment at Sky Mountain offers 1,400 square feet of thoughtfully designed living space, perfect for families or professionals seeking both comfort and convenience. With a spacious layout and modern finishes, this apartment combines practicality with elegance. The open-plan living and dining area opens to a private balcony with stunning cityscape views, allowing natural light and fresh air to fill the home.

The sleek, fully fitted kitchen makes meal preparation a joy, while the master suite offers a peaceful retreat with an attached en-suite bathroom. Residents also enjoy a host of on site amenities including a rooftop garden, gym, swimming pool, children's play area, and 24-hour security. With its unbeatable location, secure environment, and contemporary design, this apartment is an ideal choice for those looking to enjoy urban living at its finest.
